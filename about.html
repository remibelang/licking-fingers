Our Story

From the heart of West Africa to your plate in Delaware. A journey of flavor, tradition, and passion.

A Taste of Home, Away From Home

Licking Fingers was born from a simple dream: to share the rich, vibrant flavors of West African cuisine with the community of Bear, Delaware. What started as small family gatherings has grown into a beloved local restaurant.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, from the bustling markets of Lagos to the coastal kitchens of Accra. Every dish tells a story — of spice, of tradition, of love.

We believe that food is more than sustenance. It's connection. It's culture. It's the sound of laughter around a table, the warmth of a shared meal, and the joy of discovering something delicious.
